# PLAYGROUND: hallucinate a critical line, ground it, break it, learn, repeat

This repo is now a playground. Endless loop: bold invention + brutal
grounding. Breakage is FUEL, not failure — every break sharpens the next
guess. The loop never closes; resume the ledger each session.

## Load the story first
Read COUNCIL.md, MACHINE_HOW_TO_USE.md (tool + AUDIT GATE), KNOWLEDGE.md
(the graveyard AND the two real nuggets). What we already know:
- Self-verification has a 0/5 record — 5 bold claims were locally real but
  globally over-claimed. Grade your own guess adversarially.
- THE FUNNEL: a "new critical line" without ζ tends to collapse to ψ or M
  in disguise (von Mangoldt / Mertens) or to a non-prime relabeling (Farey
  totient identity). Expect it; finding HOW it collapses is the point.
- The one real thing found: √x-cancellation in primes is ARITHMETIC, not
  density — real residual ~0.7√x vs Cramér fake 2–8√x. That contrast is
  your template for what "real" looks like.
- Lex / coefficient-encoding orderings are an artifact factory
  (Kurlberg–Rosenzweig short-interval noise). Prefer coordinate-free,
  intrinsic statistics.

## The constraint
A "critical line" here = a quantity built from allowed chips (mu, M,
isprime, pi, gap, omega, bigomega, tau, phi, rad, arithmetic, log, sqrt,
mod, cumulative Σ, knobs a/b — plus any you invent) whose graph is a sharp
straight or flat line AND whose residual scaling encodes prime regularity.
NO zeta, NO ZEROS table in the construction. Different route or nothing.

## One cycle — keep them SHORT (many fast guesses beat one polished one)
1. HALLUCINATE. Invent a candidate line — or a new object / chip / ordering
   / theorem. Limitless creativity. Write the guess and WHY it could be a
   line BEFORE computing. Pre-register what confirms vs breaks it.
2. SEE IT. Render with `scripts/explore.mjs eval` + `shot`; look at the
   picture (RESIDUAL, TWIN, family planes), not only metrics — 2-D
   structure hides from scalars.
3. GROUND IT. Residual exponent θ (integrate main terms properly); Cramér
   contrast vs ≥5 fake seeds; stability of effect size r across growing
   range (NOT an endpoint, NOT z). Prime claim → composite control
   (25, 35, 77… must fail it). Equivalent claim → factor check (not a
   zero-free multiplier twist of ψ/M).
4. BREAK or SURVIVE. Most break — log exactly HOW (collapsed to ψ/M?
   non-prime? density-only? noise? main-term misfit? ordering artifact?) as
   a GRAVEYARD entry with a CONNECTION line. A survivor earns the full audit
   gate + a derivation (named classical tools, else labeled CONJECTURAL) +
   expert pack.
5. LEARN. Append what the break taught, then hallucinate again — smarter.

## Rules of the playground
- New math is encouraged: add arithmetic functions (src/core/math.js,
  ffield.js), chips, orderings, even conjecture new theorems — the AUDIT
  GATE is the reality that grounds the hallucination. Tests for every new
  primitive (hand-computed values).
- The grounding is REAL. A line is not "found" until it survives the gate.
  A sharp, well-explained graveyard IS a successful session.
- Honesty: pre-register every guess; no all-x claims from finite data;
  refutation is progress; never mix universe grades; one ledger row per
  cycle (guess → broke how / survived). Write a HANDOFF before stopping so
  the next session resumes the ledger.

Play. Break things. Learn. Guess again.
