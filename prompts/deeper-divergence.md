# GOAL: explain the divergence — the deeper structure with the breakthrough on its back

The council's thesis (COUNCIL.md): the deeper structure of the primes is
exposed at the BRIDGE between two universes — ℤ, where RH/twins/Chowla are
open, and F_q[t], where they are THEOREMS. Where the universes DIVERGE, the
mechanism governing primes is laid bare on a side we can actually prove.
We hold a real divergence. This goal does not stop at observing it again —
it stops when we UNDERSTAND it well enough to PREDICT it. A deeper structure
carries its breakthrough on its back: a mechanism that predicts is the result.

## The progress we stand on (audited, real)
The F_3[t] Möbius-gap divergence (KNOWLEDGE.md, mobius-gap-expert-pack):
Corr(μ(f−t), next_gap(f) | G_-(f)>t) ≈ 0.02, reproduced independently.
Audit established it is REAL (not a bug), present WITHOUT the conditioning
(not a collider), VANISHES under high-coefficient perturbation (it lives in
the low-order / lex short-interval neighborhood), and is NULL in F_2[t] and
ℤ. The effect is characteristic-specific — so a mechanism EXISTS. Find it.

## The deeper-structure question (the only completion)
Produce a MECHANISM that explains why this divergence exists in F_3 and not
in F_2 / ℤ, and that PREDICTS its behavior across the F_q family before you
compute each field. The lead the audit points at: odd-characteristic
structure — polynomial discriminant / Frobenius sign / quadratic residues —
which exists for odd q and degenerates at characteristic 2 (Artin–Schreier).

Completion = ALL of:
1. MECHANISM, stated precisely: what arithmetic quantity couples μ(f−t) to
   the forward gap, and why it is odd-characteristic.
2. PRE-REGISTERED PREDICTION: before computing, write the predicted sign and
   rough size for ≥3 untested fields — odd (F_5, F_7) AND characteristic-2
   (F_4 or F_8) — plus the predicted nullity that recovers F_2 and ℤ as
   special cases.
3. CONFIRMATION: compute them; predictions hold (present where predicted,
   null where predicted). Prediction is the proof of understanding — this is
   HOLDOUT IN FIELD SPACE, and a mechanism that predicts new instances is a
   breakthrough whether or not the literature names it.
4. THE LAW: state it as a clean conjecture over the q-family (effect size as
   a function of q, of q mod 4, or of whether −1 is a square), quote the
   nearest catalog result, name the difference.

If the mechanism turns out to be exactly a known short-interval Möbius
identity, say which one — that is still a real win (we now understand it) and
the campaign moves to the next divergence. A confirmed cross-q predictive
law the literature does not state is the breakthrough.

## Method
Use ffield.js. Measure effect size r, NOT z (z grows with N even as r
decays; the prior gate erred here). Every field, every time: ≥5
composite-only seeds, cyclic-shift control, and the high-coefficient placebo
(must stay null). First extend F_3 to degree ~18–20 and confirm r plateaus
rather than decays — if it decays to 0, the effect was finite-size and you
report that and pick the next divergence from KNOWLEDGE's OPEN leads.

Append universe-labeled KNOWLEDGE entries with CONNECTION lines; write a
HANDOFF before stopping. Honesty regime: pre-register every prediction in
the log BEFORE computing; a refuted mechanism is logged and replaced —
refutation is progress. No all-x claims from finite data. The exit is a
confirmed predictive mechanism (expert pack + Lean-stub the conjecture) or,
if the effect dies under range extension, the next divergence.
