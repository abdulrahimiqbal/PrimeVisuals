# STANDING GOAL: the breakthrough campaign — runs until a real one lands or escalates

This goal does NOT stop at a finding, a reformulation, or a "nice result."
It stops only when (A) a candidate clears the FULL audit gate below and is
packaged for expert review, or (B) leads are exhausted and you emit a STUCK
PACK escalating to a human number theorist. Persistent across sessions:
read COUNCIL.md, MACHINE_HOW_TO_USE.md (esp. the audit gate), KNOWLEDGE.md;
resume from the latest HANDOFF. One running log: logs/<today>-campaign.md.

## What counts as a breakthrough (the only completion)

A ⭐⭐ finding that is one of:
- a new, replicable STATISTICAL LAW of primes (LO-S / murmuration class), or
- a cross-universe SHARED LAW or DIVERGENCE between F_q[t] and ℤ at a
  matched statistic,
AND that passes EVERY audit-gate item, in writing, in the log:
 1. FACTOR CHECK — not a zero-free-multiplier twist of a catalog object
    (how L2 died).
 2. COMPOSITE CONTROL — if it mentions primes, test 25,35,49,77,91,143…;
    if any composite satisfies it, it is not a prime law (how Farey died).
    Cramér tests density, not primality — it does not substitute.
 3. ≥5-seed Cramér contrast: real beats fake.
 4. PERSISTENCE: sharpens at 2× and 4× range.
 5. HOLDOUT: survives on a disjoint range it was not found on.
 6. NOVELTY: beats the nearest catalog entry on CONTENT (quote it).
AND stated as a precise conjecture (quantity ± resolution, exponent, range)
with a `shot`, a link, and — if theorem-shaped — a derivation through named
classical tools or an honest CONJECTURAL label. No partial credit; a result
that fails any item is logged at its true grade and the campaign CONTINUES.

## Search order (highest expected value first)

Cycle per prompts/research-loop.md. Each cycle: pick the top lead, design
the decisive experiment and its null BEFORE computing, answer it fully,
append a universe-labeled KNOWLEDGE entry with a CONNECTION line, spawn ≤2
new leads, repeat.

1. TWO-UNIVERSES Sprints 2–3 (prompts/two-universes.md): calibrate Chowla
   decay / twin density against the F_q[t] theorems, then the matched
   divergence battery. Audit-safe by construction — a theorem calibrates the
   F_q[t] side. The first divergence that clears the gate is a breakthrough.
2. UNCOMPUTED cross-statistics nobody publishes: does μ(n) know gap(n)? do
   gaps differ after quadratic residues vs non-residues? second/triple
   correlations; conditional spacing laws. This is where LO-S and
   murmurations actually came from — your instrument's native edge.
3. Famous objects (ψ, M, π, twin counts) score ~0 novelty — do not
   re-measure them except as calibration.

## Why it cannot stall in self-deception

Three prior "completes" (ψ, L2, Farey) were each locally correct and
globally mis-framed, and each passed the agent's own checks. The gate
exists because self-verification has a 0/3 record here. Run it adversarially
against your OWN candidate before believing it.

## Escalation (the honest other exit)

When a cycle produces a ⭐⭐ that clears the gate, STOP and write the expert
pack (object, statement, evidence, novelty, Lean stub). When the lead queue
is genuinely exhausted without one, emit a STUCK PACK: the sharpest open
question, what was tried, what an expert could answer. Both are
deliverables; neither is failure. A robust observation's true exit is a
human mathematician.

Honesty regime always: predeclared nulls, no all-x claims from finite ℤ
data, the ranked graveyard is valid output, never mix universe grades.
